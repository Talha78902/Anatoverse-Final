About AnatoVerse Explorer

AnatoVerse Human Body Explorer is a high-fidelity interactive academic sandbox built to help students map the architectural lines, primary organs, and system operations of human biology.

The 3D environment was generated procedurally to provide a direct physical mapping of joints, skeletal ribs, nervous synapses, airways, digestive loops, cardiovascular arteries and kidneys inside a glowing holographic mannequin.
